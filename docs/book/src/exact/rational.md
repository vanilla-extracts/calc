# Section VII.1. Rational Reduction

As of version `v2.11.0` rational exact math reduction was added, it supports.

- Rational operations
  - `+`
  - `-`
  - `*`
  - `/`
- Rational reductions
- Float rationalization (with 10 digits of precision)
